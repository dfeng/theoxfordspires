### 日期

2014年8月3日-16日

### Eligibility

Students need to:

- be 16+ years old;
- have completed Year 11 and achieved at least 2 As and 3Bs at GCSE or equivalent; and
- achieved at least an overall band score of 5.0 in the IELTS or equivalent.
